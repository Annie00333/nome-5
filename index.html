Welcome to Heldorya

Enter a magical Minecraft realm filled with wonders, challenges, and camaraderie. Heldorya is a modded 1.20.1 server where adventure and creativity thrive in a medieval fantasy setting.

Spawn inside the grand Valdareth Castle, where your journey begins amidst breathtaking architecture and a warm community. Explore the lands, trade, build, and defend your honor!

Laws of the Land

  • Respect others: No griefing, harassment, or cheating.
  • Protect your builds: Use the provided land protection tools wisely.
  • Prison system: Players causing damage may be confined within the castle's underground prison cells as penalty.
  • Donations: None required; server supported by admins.
  • Modpack: Must be installed before joining. Contact admins for details.

Explore the World

Build your own house in unique biomes, buy an abode in the spawn village to live safely near friends, and enjoy the vibrant economy.

Discover the enchanted Great Raven Portal, gather at the Throne Hall, and roam the castle’s underground chambers—including cells for those who break the peace.

Heldorya offers you the freedom to create, explore, and engage in medieval life like never before.

Protection & Prisons

To maintain peace and order, players causing damage may be confined to castle prison cells as consequence of their actions. These cells are designed for roleplay and serve as a reminder to respect the community.
